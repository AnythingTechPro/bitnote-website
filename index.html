About

Digital cryptocurrencies are slowly adapting to everyday life to create a new revolutionary economy based around digital money. Bitnote allows anyone, anywhere to send money instantly around the world at nearly zero fees.

Bitnote is a digital Cryptocurrency based on the CryptoNote technology. Bitnote provides complete anonymity for users within the blockchain by using the revolutionary concept of Ring Signatures. Ring Signatures mixup transaction data with other transactions to make it nearly impossible for blockchain analysist to determine where the money came from, and who it was sent to.

Features

True Privacy:

Bitnote utilizes Ring Signatures to mask transaction data and make it nearly impossible to trace transactions. This means everyone within the Bitnote blockchain is completely anonmyous, and can send transactions worry free of blockchain analysis.


Fast Transactions:

Bitnote allows for extremely fast transactions, each block within the Bitnote blockchain will be mined in 60 seconds or less. Making transaction verification in just seconds.


Security:

Bitnote uses a peer-to-peer, decentralized consensus network making every transaction cryptographically secure. All users within the network have complete anonymity.
